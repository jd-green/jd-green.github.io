Our Vision

Dominion Equity Holdings is a holding company that builds and/or acquires businesses focused on expressing the theological principle of dominion – the divine mandate for responsible stewardship and positive transformation of society. Led by James Green alongside a diverse team of experienced operators, the company partners with business owners who share their values-driven approach, seeking to create lasting impact across various industries while maintaining high standards of operational excellence.

Through its unique approach anchored in faith-informed principles, Dominion Equity sets itself apart from traditional holding companies and private equity firms by focusing on long-term value creation that extends beyond financial returns. The company's portfolio includes businesses like Better Worship, Psalm One, and Door Renew, each contributing to its mission of exercising meaningful, positive influence in their respective sectors while building sustainable, profitable enterprises that honor the principle of dominion.
