Vision

Dominion Equity Holdings is a holding company that builds and/or acquires businesses focused on expressing the theological principle of dominion – the divine mandate for responsible stewardship and positive transformation of society.

Led by James Green alongside a diverse team of experienced operators, the company partners with business owners who share their values-driven approach, seeking to create lasting impact across various industries while maintaining high standards of operational excellence.

Through its unique approach anchored in faith-informed principles, Dominion Equity sets itself apart from traditional holding companies and private equity firms by focusing on long-term value creation that extends beyond financial returns.
